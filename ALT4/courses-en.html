Courses

Pre-Elementary

Introduce your child to English through playful songs, games, and animated videos. Optional homework handouts are available.

Elementary (1st & 2nd Grade)

Building foundational skills with interactive activities. Focus on introducing multiple viewpoints and fostering respectful listening.

Elementary (3rd & 4th Grade)

Develop your child’s ability to form independent opinions through group discussions and interactive projects. Mandatory homework and textbooks.

Elementary (5th & 6th Grade)

Advanced language skills with basic debate techniques. Emphasis on critical thinking and presentation skills.

Junior High School

Engage in practical English discussions and test preparation. Learn to express and respect diverse viewpoints.

High School

Prepare for advanced discussions and debates. Focus on building confidence and extroverted communication skills.

University Students

Develop complex opinions and engage in high-level discussions. Intensive test preparation and academic English.

Parents Course

Learn strategies to support your child’s independent thinking and English learning. Tips for effective home practice.

Business English

Enhance your professional communication skills. Includes TOEIC preparation, business vocabulary, and real-world discussions.

Senior Citizens

Maintain cognitive agility through conversation, group activities, and cultural discussions. A social club for engaging discussions.
